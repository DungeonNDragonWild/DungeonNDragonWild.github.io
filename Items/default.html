The Black Sword

The sword passed down for generations of Dorwine's royalty, this gigantic sword serves as a symbol. Not only of strength, but also power. [PREREQ] Wielder is required to have a STR of 18, otherwise the sword will be too unwieldy to use in combat [STATS] Base attack: 3D6 (max damage modifier of +2) Special Action: Black Swing - Swing this sword in a big arc and hit up to 3 people in front of you (5 Ft) (Twice per short rest)
